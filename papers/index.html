How to use. Click QP to open the question paper, MS for the mark scheme, INS for the insert (Econ P3 extracts). Opens in a new tab so you can read both side-by-side.

June 2024 papers are not yet public — they come out of embargo twelve months after the exam series. AQA typically releases them mid-June.

Strategy tip. Don't cram past papers. Do them under time pressure, closed book. Mark honestly using the mark scheme. Then spend 2× as long reviewing wrong answers as you did doing the paper. The review is where learning happens.
